About me

Hello my name is Macy, I am 19 years old studying Media production at De Montfort Universtiy. After reading the title you are all probably thinking Why am I going to read about about someone being born, surely it is the same as everyone else after 9 months. I can assure you me coming into the world was definetley not normal and my parents did not have the most wonderful experience either.

I was very premature, My due date was originally December 10th, I suppose I was very excited to come into the world and I was born August 24th 2001 on my mums birthday down a toilet- Thankfully my mum caught me. So on my mums 32nd birthday she spent it in hospital trying to persuade the nurses that she was in labour, went to the toilet, I flew out, she (Thankfully) caught me.
