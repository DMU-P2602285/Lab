The Cast

The Perks of Being a Wallflower. cast.

On this page I am going to talk to you about the different cast members in this film. The actors who were playing the mian characters were Emma Watson as Sam, Logan Lerman as Charlie, Ezra Miller as Patrick and Mae whitman as Mary Elizabeth.

Emma Watson-Sam

Emma Watson is mostly known for playing the role of 'Hermoine Granger' in Harry potter. After Harry Potter Emma attended Brown university in the states to persue a normal life, it was not until a few months later where she got sent a script through her agent and fell in love with the novel. She attended auditions and managed to get the role of Sam.

Logan Lerman-Charlie

Logan was born into a jewish family. At only 2 years old Logan told his mum that he wanted to be actor, and at only 4 years old Logan had an agent and was booked for 2 commercials. By 2000 he made a debut in 'The Pariot'. Since then he was in many films and by the age of 20 he recieved the role as Charlie.

Ezra Miller-Patrick

Ezra Miller was born in New jersey. As a child Ezra sang with the Metropolitan Opera and attended the rockland country day schoo and hudson schoo. In 2008 Ezra featured in Afterschool. Since then Ezra has been in many films- but it wasn't until 2012 where he played Patrick in The Perks of Being a Wallflower.
